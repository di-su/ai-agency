Mortgage Broker Workflow Comparison

Manual Workflow (Before Automation)

1. Lead Capture
- Manually check emails/DMs for new leads.
- Info often incomplete or messy.
- Reply manually asking standard questions.
- Manually create entry in spreadsheet/basic CRM.
Pain Points: Slow, repetitive, inconsistent data, time-consuming.
2. Pre-Qualification & Initial Contact
- Review basic info provided (if any).
- Manually email a checklist of required documents.
- Decide if lead is worth pursuing.
Pain Points: Back-and-forth emails, delays, manual assessment.
3. Document Collection
- Manually track who sent what.
- Chase clients via email/phone for missing docs.
- Download attachments from emails.
- Manually sort/rename files into folders (or desktop chaos).
Pain Points: Huge time sink, frustrating, disorganized, delays deals.
4. Application & Quoting
- Manually enter client data into lender portals.
- Search for suitable mortgage products.
- Manually type up recommendation emails/summaries.
Pain Points: Data re-entry, time spent writing similar emails.
5. Follow-up & Updates
- Manually check deal status.
- Manually email clients with updates (or forget).
- Respond to "where are we?" queries individually.
- Manual appointment scheduling.
- No systematic post-deal follow-up.
Pain Points: Time-consuming, reactive, missed opportunities for future business.

AI-Automated Workflow (After)

1. Lead Capture & Intake
- Lead fills out smart online form (Tally/Typeform).
- Data automatically sent to CRM (Airtable/Notion) via Zapier/Make.
- AI generates quick summary for broker.
- Auto-sends professional welcome email with next steps.
Benefits: Instant, consistent, professional, data captured cleanly.
2. Pre-Qualification & Doc Request
- Form collects all necessary initial info.
- Automated email/SMS sent with personalized document checklist.
- Secure upload link provided (e.g., to Google Drive).
Benefits: Faster qualification, clear instructions, streamlined process.
3. Automated Document Collection
- CRM (Airtable) tracks received/missing docs.
- Automated email/SMS reminders sent (via Zapier + Twilio) for missing docs.
- Files automatically named/sorted into client's Google Drive folder.
Benefits: Saves hours of chasing, keeps deals moving, organized files.
4. Application & Quoting Assist
- Clean data ready in CRM for easier input to lender portals.
- AI assist (ChatGPT/Zapier AI) drafts summary/recommendation emails based on templates.
- Broker reviews & sends with minor edits.
Benefits: Faster communication, consistent messaging.
5. Automated Follow-up & Updates
- Automated status updates triggered by CRM stage changes.
- Easy scheduling via integrated link (Calendly/TidyCal).
- Automated reminders for appointments.
- Post-completion thank you & automated future review reminders.
Benefits: Proactive communication, less manual work, builds client loyalty, generates future leads.
